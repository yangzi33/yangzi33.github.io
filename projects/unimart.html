An eBay-like online item trading web application. An user can be either a seller or a buyer.

  • Users may place item listings with images, descriptions, and the pricing.
  • If users are interested in particular listings, they may contact the sellers through a built-in messaging system.
  • (An upcoming feature) Users may choose to pay deposits through online payment gateways such as PayPal.

We wish to restrict the trades for students, in around school areas, to reduce potential risks during face-to-face item tradings. Therefore, we are currently developing an verification system, to ensure that the users are current students.

Frameworks used: SpringMVC, MyBatis, Angular
Some tools involved: Intellij IDEA, Apache Maven, PostgreSQL
